@Memorang is building the AI tech stack for education, providing organizations with an integrated suite of tools to build, launch, and scale AI-powered education solutions. Our platform includes:

- **EdWrite**: A powerful content management system with AI assistance for content creation and evaluation
- **EdFlow**: Workflow automation tools for educational content development
- **AILA**: Intelligent learning assistance with context-aware hints and feedback
- **Knowledge Graph UI**: Visual content organization and relationship mapping

Through these tools, we enable education businesses to leverage AI while maintaining focus on their core mission of delivering high-quality learning experiences.
