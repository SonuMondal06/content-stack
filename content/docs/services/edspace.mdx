# EdSpace: Complete EdTech Monetization

## Overview
EdSpace is your monetization layer for EdTech - an integrated suite of tools to manage and monetize your apps.

## Business Tools
- Custom product configuration with gated features and entitlements
- Flexible subscription plans and pricing options
- Unified dashboards for tracking customers, orders and subscriptions

## Integration & Support
- Marketing automation integrations (Mixpanel, OneSignal, Resend)
- Native payment integrations (Apple, Google, Stripe)
- E-commerce connectors
- AI-powered customer support with Hub Copilot
