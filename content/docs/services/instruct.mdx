An AI-first learning management system (LMS) that reimagines the relationships between instructors and students through AI-powered teaching assistants (AiTAs).

## Platform Benefits
Transform traditional classroom experiences with personalized, scalable learning solutions.

## Key Features
- AI Teaching Assistants (AiTAs)
- Personalized learning paths
- Scalable classroom management
